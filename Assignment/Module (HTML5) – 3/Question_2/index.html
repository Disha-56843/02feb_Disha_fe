Q:2 How to embed audio and video in a webpage.


There is three ways to embed audio and video in a webpage.

  1. Embed Audio:
    Audio tag is use to embed any audio in a webpage.
    There is three audio formats - MP3, WAV, OGG. Only Safari browser doesn't support OGG audio format.

    Here,

    controls - audio control such as play, pause, and stop.
    autoplay - play audio as soon as possible.
    muted - When the page is loaded audio will be automatically muted.
    loop - It will start the audio again when it is finished.
    Example:

    Syntax:

    <audio src="" type=""></audio>

    OR

    <audio controls muted autoplay loop>
      <source src="" type="" >
    </audio>

    Note:

    Above syntax is use when you have any downloaded audio or video. If you don't have any downloaded audio or video than you can below syntax.

    <embed src="" type="">

    OR

    Using iframe
  2. Embed Video:
    Video tag is use to embed any video in a webpage.
    There is three video formats - MP3, WAV, OGG. Only Safari browser doesn't support OGG video format.

    Here,

    controls - video control such as play, pause, and stop.
    autoplay - play video as soon as possible.
    muted - When the page is loaded video will be automatically muted.
    loop - It will start the video again when it is finished.
    poster - an image will be shown until video play.
    Example:

    Syntax:

    <video src="" type=""></video>

    OR

    <video controls muted autoplay loop>
      <source src="" type="" >
    </video>

    Note:

    Above syntax is use when you have any downloaded audio or video. If you don't have any downloaded audio or video than you can below syntax.

    <embed src="" type="">;

    OR

    Using iframe
